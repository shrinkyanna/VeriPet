The System

How VeriPet Works

01

Report & Document

Public submits photos, videos, and reports. Every file is auto-timestamped with full chain-of-custody records.

02

Verify & Investigate

Cases are prepared and made available to verified investigators and legal partners to build high-trust evidence files.

03

Enforce & Escalate

VeriPet supplies evidence and expert testimony to support enforcement actions and permanent bans.
